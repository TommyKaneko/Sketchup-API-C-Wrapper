43/**
44 * @brief Wrapper for SUGroupRef.
45 *
46 * A group is a special type of component instance that is always unique.
47 * Groups inherit from ComponentInstance and provide direct access to their
48 * entities, name, and transformation.
49 *
50 * @note Groups must always be attached to a parent. There is no release
51 * function for groups in the C API.
