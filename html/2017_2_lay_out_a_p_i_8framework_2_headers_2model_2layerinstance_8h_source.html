9 /**
10 @struct LOLayerInstanceRef
11 @brief References a layer instance. A layer instance provides access to the
12  entities that are drawn on a given layer, as well as the draw order of
13  those entities. Groups are not included in the list of entities
14  associated with a layer instance, since the group hierarchy has no
15  effect on entity draw order. Each page has one layer instance for each
16  layer in the document. Non-shared layer instances are unique per page,
17  while shared layer instances are shared across all pages of the
18  document.
