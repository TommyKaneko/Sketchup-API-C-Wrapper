42/**
43 * @brief Wrapper for SUTransformation, representing a 4x4
44 * affine transformation matrix.

46 * Provides constructors from translation, rotation, scale, and
47 * various combinations, along with arithmetic operators for

50 * @see https://extensions.sketchup.com/developer_center/sketchup_c_api/
